Chicken Cordon Bleu

Description

Ham and cheese stuffed chicken breast coated in paprika and white wine sauce

Ingredients

  • boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • swiss cheese slices
  • ham slices
  • all-purpose flour
  • paprika
  • butter
  • dry white wine
  • chicken bouillon
  • cornstarch
  • heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Place a slice of cheese and ham on each chicken breast within 1/2 of the edges. Fold the edges of the chicken over the filling and secure with toothpicks
  2. Mix 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour and 1 tablespoon of paprika in a small bowl and coat chicken pieces
  3. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat and cook chicken until browned on all sides. Add the wine and bouillon
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is no longer pink and juices fun clear.
  5. Remove the toothpicks and transfer the breasts to a warm platter
  6. Blend the cornstarch and cream in a small bowl and wisk slowly into the skillet. stir until thickened and pour over chicken. Serve warm
