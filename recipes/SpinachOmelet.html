Spinach Omelet

Description

Spinach Omelet with tender baby greens topped with parmesan and nutmeg

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • torn baby spinach leaves
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • onion powder
  • ground nutmeg
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl, stir in baby spinach and parmesan. Season with onion powder, nutmeg, salt and pepper
  2. Spray small skillet with cooking spray and place over medium heat. Once warm add in egg mixture and cook until partially set, approximately 3 minutes
  3. Flip with spatula and continue cooking 2 to 3 minutes
  4. reduce heat to low and continue cooking to desired effect
